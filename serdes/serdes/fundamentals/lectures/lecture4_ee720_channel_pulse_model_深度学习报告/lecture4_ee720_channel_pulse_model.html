Nyquist Frequency • Nyquist bandwidth constraint: • The theoretical minimum required system bandwidth to detect RS (symbols/s) without ISI is RS/2 (Hz)

• Thus, a system with bandwidth W=1/2T=RS/2 (Hz) can support a maximum transmission rate of 2W=1/T=RS (symbols/s) without ISI 1 R R  S  W  S  2 (symbols/s/Hz) 2T 2 W
