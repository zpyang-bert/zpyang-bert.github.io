OIF CEI Linear Interop Demo – It Works!

This interoperability demo consists of a 112G Linear test board receiving and converting an optical signal across single mode fiber being transmitted by a remote-laser-source-driven optical engine to an electrical signal via PD+TIA. The electrical signal is then sent to a BERT which integrates receive FFE functionality and achieves a link BER exceeding standard requirements.
